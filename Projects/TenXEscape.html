Date: May 2023
I programmed this for my final project in Advanced Computer Programming. One of my favorite games as a kid was 40x Escape by Bart Bonte. So, when the opportunity presented itself in the form of making a more advanced GUI with Maven and JavaFX, I made my own mini-version, complete with faithful renditions of some of its most iconic levels, some of my own ideas, and the beautiful credits screen. The most challenging part was getting the GUIs to interact and switch to one another, and after that it was figuring out the logic and effects. The above youtube link is me playing through it, and the repo link has a solutions guide (you should totally check it out!).
