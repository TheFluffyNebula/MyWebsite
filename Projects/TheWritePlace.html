Date: June to October 2022
This is by far and away the largest project I've ever been a part of. In May 2022, we pitched the idea for the Ready, Set, App! Contest, of which we got 3rd place. My best friend Eric Wang and I wanted to see how far we could take the app, We worked on the Android side during the summer, and I made the website side during the school year which can be seen in the repo. We used webpack to put it all together. I was actually planning to submit the Builder Base Guide Website for my final project for my Web Development class but my professor and I decided it was too simple. Thus, I went with this instead, and 200+ commits later, the result is beautiful but not secure at all.

DO NOT ENTER ANY SENSITIVE INFORMATION IF YOU DECIDE TO TEST IT

The app and website can create users that can interact with each other and give feedback on essays. They can also query based on tags. There's a review, upload, and feedback section. The authentication and database are done through firebase, as shown below. I hope to learn enough about cybersecurity to go back one day and make it secure enough for schools to use.
