Date: August to December 2022
I programmed this out of pure passion: no assignment due, just some free time. I made this as a guide to help my friends for the video game Clash of Clash, mainly the Builder Base (aka Night Village) mode. Though this mode has been changed to where it's unrecognizable, I have fond memories of it. It represents the first time I knew that I was really good at something. Below are some of my accomplishments, most notably finishing #36 globally and #1 in the US in April of 2022.

I actually made this as soon as I learned that one could put an image inside an anchor tag. That day I got home and pumped out the first twenty pages' skeletons in that one night. I didn't get to sharing it around a lot, but I can say that a few friends from the Netherlands used it with decent results :-)

Fun fact: this is the website whose CSS this one is based on!
